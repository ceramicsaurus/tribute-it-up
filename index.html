PLOT

Dead Boy Detectives follows Charles Rowland and Edwin Paine, two teenage boys who die tragically but choose to remain on Earth as ghostly detectives, solving supernatural mysteries. Charles died of hypothermia, and Edwin was murdered decades earlier at the same boarding school. Together, they use their spectral abilities to investigate paranormal cases while grappling with their own unresolved trauma and connection to the afterlife.

As they face dangerous otherworldly forces, the boys must also confront their decision to stay on Earth, uncovering deeper emotional truths about themselves. The show mixes dark humor, horror, and detective elements in a unique narrative of friendship and loss.
